# Privacy Policy for Horo

**Effective Date**: December 23, 2024
**Last Updated**: December 23, 2024

---

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Horo ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS fitness coaching application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Health and Fitness Data

We collect workout data from Apple Health (HealthKit) when you authorize access:

**Running**:
- Distance, pace, duration
- Heart rate, cadence
- Vertical oscillation, step length
- Calories burned

**Cycling**:
- Distance, speed, duration
- Power (NP, FTP, IF), RPM
- Calories burned

**Swimming**:
- Distance, duration
- SWOLF score, stroke count, lap count
- Calories burned

**Walking**:
- Distance, pace, duration
- Step count, heart rate
- Calories burned

**Collection**:
- Data is collected **only** when you authorize HealthKit access
- Data is collected **only** for workouts you choose to analyze
- You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings

### User Preferences

- Fitness goal (performance, weight loss, health)
- Fitness level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Main sport preference (running, cycling, swimming, walking)
- Selected coach preference
- Language preference (English or French)

### Device Information

- Persistent device identifier (UUID stored in Keychain)
- App version
- iOS version

### Subscription Data

- Apple receipt data for in-app purchase verification
- Subscription status (free or premium)
- Subscription expiration date

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Coaching Services

- Analyze your workout metrics to generate personalized coaching feedback
- Adapt coaching style based on your selected coach
- Provide localized feedback in your preferred language
- Track your progress and adapt recommendations

### To Manage Your Subscription

- Verify in-app purchases with Apple
- Track daily analysis quota (2 per day for free users)
- Enable premium features for subscribers

### To Improve Our Service

- Understand how users interact with the app
- Improve AI coaching quality
- Fix bugs and technical issues

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### With Our Backend Service

- Your workout metrics are sent to our secure backend API for AI analysis
- Your device ID is used to manage subscription and quota
- Your preferences are used to personalize coaching
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

### With Third-Party Services

**OpenAI**:
- Your workout metrics are sent to OpenAI's API (via our backend) to generate coaching feedback
- No personal identifying information is shared
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies: [https://openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy)

**Apple**:
- Subscription receipts are verified with Apple's servers
- Apple's privacy policy applies: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (On Your Device)

- Coach selections and preferences are stored locally using SwiftData
- Coaching comments are cached locally on your device
- Your device identifier is stored securely in your device's Keychain
- Local data is encrypted by iOS

### Backend Storage (On Our Servers)

- Workout metrics are temporarily processed and not permanently stored
- Subscription status and quota are stored on our secure servers
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Servers are located in secure data centers

### Data Retention

- **Cached comments**: Remain on your device until you delete the app
- **Subscription data**: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 1 year
- **Workout metrics**: Processed in real-time, not stored long-term
- You can request deletion of your data at any time

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### Access to HealthKit

- You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time
- Go to: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Horo
- Revoking access will prevent the app from analyzing new workouts

### Data Deletion

**Local Data**:
- Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
- Your HealthKit data remains safely in Apple Health

**Backend Data**:
- Email us at [YOUR SUPPORT EMAIL] to request deletion
- We will delete your data within 30 days

### Subscription Management

- Manage or cancel your subscription in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
- Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple

### Do Not Sell My Data

- We do not sell personal data
- We do not share data with advertisers
- Your data is used only to provide coaching services
